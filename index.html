SmartRaise

Making Donations Smarter!

What We Do

SmartRaise is a nonprofit organization that uses blockchain technology and cryptocurrency to enable users to donate directly to organizations.

The Problem

Every year, Nonprofits struggle to raise donations that are crucial for their foundation. In addition, Donors struggle to find reliable nonprofits to donate to.

Our Solution

Our technology enables users to donate directly to nonprofits. Through the power of blockchain technology donors know that their donations are being put to a good cause.
